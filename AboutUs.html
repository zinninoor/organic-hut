About Organic Hut Dairy Farm

Organic Hut is a venture by Mr. Sumit Sharma & family, at Village Hansali, District Fatehgarh Sahib, Punjab started in the year 2014.

The production of milk in the whole wide world is entirely derived from and is dependent upon Dairy Farm Animals like Cattle's. The presence and importance of these species is very significant in the growth of all economies of every nation.

Having direct impact on the lives of people, Dairy Farm Animals should to be properly cared and well-maintained, so as to ensure optimal production of milk. Not just limited to this, they should be fed with good quality, highly nutritious food that can easily get digested and must be vaccinated to keep them healthy. Such is our herd of Dairy Farm Animals that possess and are best featured for all the aforesaid factors. We, Tiwana Dairy Farm are a leading Trader and Supplier of all kinds of Cattle Breed in India. of HF, Jersey, Sahiwal & Tharparkar Cows and Mehsana & Murrah Buffalos. These animals are kept in the most perfect way and cared like babies by our talented team of professionals.

For the purpose of producing naturally grown produce, we as Organic farmers have not left our farm to be taken over by nature. We use all the knowledge, techniques and materials available to work with nature. In this way we create a healthy balance between nature and farming, where crops and animals can grow and thrive.

Our natural and Integrated Organic farming also involves a good use of animal husbandry, as we have Holstein Friesian for A1 Cow Milk and 'Desi' Sahiwal cows for A2 Cow Milk, & free range chickens at our farm as well. For the dairy farming & goats, we provide Organic naturally grown fodder to the livestock grown at the farm itself, & it is our effort to keep the greens of the livestock completely toxin free, with a very careful watch on the vetenary inputs as well, so that for treatment of normally occurring ailments, more than necessary medicines and chemicals are not given to the livestock. This further greatly improves the quality of milk.Mrs. Kiran Gill, daughter in law of Mr. Sukhchain S Gill has also completed a course on Dairy Farming conducted by the Punjab Dairy Development board in the year 2014 under the Women Empowerment Scheme at Dairy Training & Extension Centre at Chatamli.

In order to be a successful in our organic farming efforts, we don't see every insect as a pest, every plant out of place as a weed and the solution to every problem in an artificial chemical spray. The aim is not to eradicate all pests and weeds, but to keep them down to an acceptable level and make the most of the benefits that they may provide. We also use various combined techniques of natural farming. At our farm, each technique is not normally used on its own. We use the method of integrated farming where many disciplines o f farming are practiced at the same time & allow them to work together for the maximum benefit, as each is complimentary to the other. For example the dairy & goats provide the fields with manure & in turn the fields provide organic fodder for them & therefore both activities benefit from each other. This way we grow wheat (C 306), wheat, Basmati Rice, Pulses (like various dal's, Chana etc.) & Fresh seasonal Vegetables. We also have a multi fruit Orchard at our farm.

We are certified Organic by SGS India limited, through the Punjab Agri-Export Corporation, which is the nodal agency for Organic Certification by Government of Punjab.

We have also started with Eco-Tourism for Farm Stays at our farm, for guests to stay with us in comfortable double occupancy suites, & experience life at a working Organic farm. Fresh, home made organic meals are also provided to guests made from the home grown produce.
